Mission

Seton Fund's mission is to ensure that every child, regardless of financial background, has access to a traditional education that supports their academic, social, and personal growth. Through financial support, educational resources, and community partnerships, we aim to empower families to give their children the opportunity to thrive in a traditional academic setting.

Vision

Our vision is to create a world where traditional education is not a privilege, but a universal right. We envision a future where every child has the support they need to pursue their educational goals, attend a school of their choice, and reach their full potential in life - while encouraging their personal networks and communities to participate in supporting them.

Our Families

Seton Fund targets all families seeking traditional education for their children, with a primary focus on families in and around the greater Madison, WI area. We serve anyone looking to afford traditional education for their children.

Programs & Services

529 Tuition Payment Program

Seton Fund establishes a 529 account for each student seeking financial help to attend private schools. Funds distributed to each student's individual 529 can be applied to tuition, school supplies, uniforms, extracurricular activities, and other educational costs.

Learn More

Application & Enrollment Assistance

For families looking for assistance with application and enrollment processes, we provide comprehensive guidance to help navigate the complex admissions process for traditional education institutions.

Learn More

Financial Vehicle Setup

For each student, Seton Fund sets up a 529 that serves as the student's account for all tuition and school-related expenses. The Fund deposits as close to 100% of the cost of their annual education expenses as possible.

Learn More

Community Participation Workshops

We offer workshops for parents to better understand financial planning for education, educate parents on how to use the 529 to garner further support from their individual communities, and maximize the value of traditional education.

Learn More

Objectives

1. Provide Financial Support

Offer tuition assistance to families to cover tuition, fees, educational materials, and ancillary school-related activities.

2. Educational Advocacy

Advocate for traditional education models and promote access to quality schooling, regardless of financial status.

3. Community Engagement

Build a network of supporters, including individuals, corporations, and educational institutions, to create a sustainable resource pool for funding.

4. Empower Families

Provide resources to help families understand the application and admissions process, offer financial tools for crowd-sourcing contributions, and set children up with access to traditional education with financial independence.

Apply for Support

Ready to take the next step? Complete our application to be considered for financial assistance through our 529 tuition payment program.

Application Process

  1. Complete the online application form with family and student information
  2. Submit required documentation including financial information
  3. Attend a brief interview to discuss educational goals and needs
  4. Receive decision within 2-3 weeks
  5. Upon approval, we'll set up your child's individual 529 account

Eligibility

  • Families seeking traditional education for their children
  • Primary focus on Madison, WI area families
  • Demonstrated financial need
  • Commitment to traditional education values

Impact & Measurement

Measuring Success

To measure the success of Seton Fund, we track the following key metrics:

  • Number of families served: Total number of families receiving funding
  • Academic outcomes: Self-reported tracking of academic performance and graduation rates for students supported by the fund
  • Financial assistance distribution: Monitor the amount of funding allocated to families for educational expenses
  • Community feedback: Gather testimonials and feedback from families and schools to ensure our services meet their needs
