Mission

Seton Fund ensures every child, regardless of financial background, can access a traditional education that fosters academic, social, and personal growth. We empower families through financial aid, resources, and community support.

Vision

Our vision is a world where traditional education is a right, not a privilegeâ€”where every child has the support to attend the school of their choice and reach their full potential, with help from their community.

Our Families

Seton Fund targets all families seeking traditional education for their children, with a primary focus on families in and around the greater Madison, WI area.

Programs & Services

529 Tuition Payment Program

Seton Fund sets up a 529 plan account for each student, covering tuition, supplies, uniforms, activities, and other school expenses.

Application & Enrollment Assistance

We guide families through the application and enrollment process for traditional education schools.

Community Participation Workshops

We offer workshops to help parents plan, use 529 accounts effectively, and maximize the value of traditional education.

Objectives

1. Provide Financial Support

Offer tuition assistance to families to cover tuition, fees, educational materials, and ancillary school-related activities.

2. Educational Advocacy

Advocate for traditional education models and promote access to quality schooling, regardless of financial status.

3. Community Engagement

Build a network of supporters, including individuals, corporations, and educational institutions, to create a sustainable resource pool for funding.

4. Empower Families

We provide families with admissions support, crowd-funding tools, and pathways to affordable traditional education.

Apply for Support

Ready to take the next step? Complete our application to be considered for financial assistance through our 529 tuition payment program.

Application Process

  1. Complete the online application form with family and student information
  2. Submit required documentation including financial information
  3. Attend a brief interview to discuss educational goals and needs
  4. Receive decision within 2-3 weeks
  5. Upon approval, we'll set up your child's individual 529 account

Eligibility

  • Families seeking traditional education for their children
  • Primary focus on Madison, WI area families
  • Demonstrated financial need
  • Commitment to traditional education values
